AmbientLight

AmbientLight is a type of light in the Three.js library that represents ambient (or global) light in a scene. Ambient light is a type of non-directional light that illuminates all objects in the scene equally, regardless of their position and orientation. It simulates the overall brightness or color tone of a scene and helps to eliminate completely dark areas
